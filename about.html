About Us

Your trusted partner in medical transportation

Committed to Your Care

K Poku Transport LLC is a dedicated non-emergency medical transportation provider serving patients, families, hospitals, and healthcare facilities. We understand that reliable transportation is essential to maintaining your health and well-being.

Our mission is to provide safe, punctual, and compassionate transportation services that put patients first. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare, and reliable transportation should never be a barrier.

With years of experience in the medical transportation industry, we've built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and compassionate care. Our team is committed to making every journey safe, comfortable, and stress-free.
